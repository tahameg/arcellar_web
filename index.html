Arcellar Demo Collection
The goal of this collection is to provide a set of basic and instructive examples of arcellar supported use cases. AR.js. Advanced three.js examples are also included.

Viewing the AR examples will require a device with a camera and the our special marker arcellar_marker(either printed or displayed on a second device).


Marker Tracking

A basic scene that shows simple marker tracking feature.


Marker Tracking(Better Marker)

A basic scene that shows simple marker tracking feature. However this one uses a different marker which is letterA. This one offers a better tracking quality
